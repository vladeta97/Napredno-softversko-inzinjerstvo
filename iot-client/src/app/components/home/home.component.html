Chichago Beach Monitoring

Chichago Beach Monitoring platform is distributed system which tracks, collects and analyzes data gathered from three most popular and tranding beaches on lake Michigan in Chichago city.

Platform controls multiple sensors on each of Foster, Oak and 63rd street beaches and has command interfaces which automatically performs isues commands and performs actions which our AI system(heart of this platform) has calculated.

This system represents part of IoT series of smart city IoT projects which will make city of Chichago a better and more interesting place to live in. Regards from Chichago Beach Monitoring developer team and always remember:"Life Is a Beach!".
